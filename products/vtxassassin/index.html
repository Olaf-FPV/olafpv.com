--- layout: product title: VTX Assassin ---
Never worry about powering up your quad while others are flying again.

The VTX Assassin adds a electrical and physical video transmitter kill switch to your quad. With convienent solder pads and ultra-clean mounting, it's a mandatory component for everyone.

Discontinuation Notice

The VTX Assassin has become obsolete due to flight controllers having integrated video transmitter kill switches. OlaFPV no longer supports or produces the VTX Assassin.
